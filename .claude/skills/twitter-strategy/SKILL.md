# X / Twitter Strategy for Hardo App

Build a repeatable posting engine for X. Always apply `hardo-brand`.

## Content pillars (map every post)

| Pillar | % | Purpose | Example |
|--------|---|---------|---------|
| **Teach** (technical/behavioral) | 35% | Authority, bookmarks | "Equity vs enterprise value in one tweet" |
| **Demystify the process** | 20% | Reach, relatability | "What banks actually screen in round 1" |
| **Confidence / mindset** | 15% | Replies, relatability | "The freeze is normal. Here's how to recover." |
| **Proof / outcomes** | 15% | Conversion | candidate wins (real only), before/after |
| **Build-in-public / product** | 15% | Followers, signups | shipping updates, question-bank peeks |

~80% value / ~20% product. Even product tweets should teach or entertain.

## Cadence (X rewards volume more than LinkedIn)

- **Starting cadence:** 1-3 tweets/day + 1-2 threads/week. Consistency compounds.
- **Best windows for a US/UK finance/student audience:** weekday mornings
  (pre-market ~7-9am ET) and evenings; markets-open hours have high FinTwit activity.
- **Reply budget:** 20-40 min/day replying to relevant accounts (this is growth, not
  optional — see `twitter-growth`).
- Threads do well when people have time to read: mid-morning & evenings, Tue-Thu.

## Tweet vs thread mix (weekly)

- ~10-15 single tweets (opinions, tips, engagement Qs, build-in-public).
- 1-2 threads (the authority pieces — technical breakdowns, "X questions" lists).
- A handful of quote-tweets/replies to ride existing conversations.

## Editorial calendar

When asked, output:
```
| Day | Pillar | Format (tweet/thread/QT) | Hook / working title | CTA | Status |
```
- Default **1-week** (X moves fast) or **2-week** view.
- Tie to the **IB recruiting calendar** (Aug-Oct peak applications = peak demand;
  Jan-Mar off-cycle/full-time). Note timing rationale in 2-3 bullets.
- Reserve 1-2 evergreen threads to re-run each recruiting cycle.

## Campaign planning

For a push (e.g. "Recruiting Season on FinTwit"):
1. Objective + one metric (profile-driven signups, or follower target).
2. Hero thread as the centerpiece; supporting tweets seed and resurface it.
3. Arc: problem-aware tweets → teach thread → proof → clear offer.
4. Distribution: founder account + engaging the FinTwit multipliers + pinned tweet.
5. Measure with `twitter-analytics`.

## Account setup levers

- **Pinned tweet:** your best thread or a crisp "what is Hardo + free mock" tweet.
- **Bio:** outcome-first ("Helping future analysts walk into IB interviews already
  prepared. Free mock 👇") + link.
- **Header image:** value prop + Hardo (use `design`/`ui-ux-pro-max`).
- For an early product, the **founder account out-performs a brand account** — lead
  personal, amplify from there.

## Brand-voice review

Check drafts against `hardo-brand`:
- ✅ Sharp hook in first 5 words? ✅ One idea, tight? ✅ Technically correct?
- ✅ Maps to a pillar + rotated CTA? ✅ Link in reply, not main tweet?
- ✅ No fabricated numbers/quotes, no job guarantees, no cringe?
Return a ✅/⚠️ checklist + a tightened rewrite.
