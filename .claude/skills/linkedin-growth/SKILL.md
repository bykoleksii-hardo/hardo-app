# LinkedIn Growth & Promotion for Hardo App

Distribution playbook. Great content + zero distribution = zero results. Always
apply `hardo-brand`.

## 1. Profile optimization (do this first)

Founder profile is the storefront:
- **Headline:** outcome-oriented, not title-oriented.
  e.g. "Helping future analysts walk into IB interviews already prepared | Building Hardo".
- **Featured section:** pin the best carousel + a "Run a free mock" link.
- **About:** the origin story + who it's for + soft CTA.
- **Banner:** clear value prop + Hardo.
Use `ui-ux-pro-max` / `design` skills for banner and featured visuals.

## 2. Engagement-led growth (the real engine)

Reach on LinkedIn is earned in **comments**, not just posts:
- **The 10-before-and-after rule:** before you post, leave 10 thoughtful comments
  on relevant posts (finance creators, recruiters, student leaders). After posting,
  reply to every comment within the first hour.
- **Comment where your audience already is:** posts by IB recruiters, finance
  influencers, university finance societies, "breaking into Wall Street" creators.
  Add value, don't pitch.
- **Reply > like.** Conversations signal the algorithm and build relationships.
- **DMs (human, not spammy):** when someone engages meaningfully, follow up with a
  genuine message and a *helpful* free resource (not a hard pitch).

## 3. Community & multiplier outreach (highest leverage)

The **Insider/Multiplier** persona (society leaders, career-services, club admins)
puts Hardo in front of whole cohorts:
- Build a target list of **university finance / investment societies**, trading
  clubs, and career centers.
- Offer genuine value first: a free practice set, a co-branded "interview prep
  night", free access for their members during recruiting season.
- Draft outreach as **short, personalized, value-first DMs/emails** (give me the
  org and I'll write them). No mass-spam.

## 4. Partnerships & collaborations

- **Micro-influencers** in finance-career content (often students/recent analysts
  with engaged niche audiences > big generic accounts). Co-create a carousel or a
  "they tried Hardo" post.
- **Guest content swaps**, comment collaborations, joint live sessions / audio events.
- **Always disclose** sponsored/partner content (compliance + trust).

## 5. Hashtag & discoverability tactics

- 3-5 niche hashtags per post (see `linkedin-content`).
- Encourage **saves and comments** explicitly ("save this for your prep") — these
  weight reach more than likes.
- Repurpose winners: turn a high-performing post into a carousel, a video, and a
  newsletter angle (use the `blog`/`blog-repurpose` skills).

## 6. Paid promotion (optional, later)

Only after organic shows what resonates:
- Boost **proven** organic posts (don't pay to find product-market-message fit).
- LinkedIn targeting that fits: students/recent grads, fields of study (finance,
  economics, accounting), member groups (finance societies), seniority = entry.
- Start tiny, measure cost per signup, kill losers fast.
- Honest creative only — no job-outcome guarantees.

## 7. 30-day growth sprint (give this when asked "how do I start")

- **Week 1:** optimize profile; pick 15 accounts/communities to engage daily; post 3x.
- **Week 2:** publish first hero carousel; start multiplier outreach (5 societies).
- **Week 3:** first partnership/collab; double down on the format that's working.
- **Week 4:** review metrics (`linkedin-analytics`), cut what flopped, plan next month.

## Output format

For growth requests, return a **prioritized, concrete action list** (what to do this
week), with any outreach copy drafted and ready to send. Tie tactics back to the
three personas and the recruiting calendar.
