# X / Twitter Analytics & Reporting for Hardo App

Measure what moves the goal (signups & qualified followers), not vanity likes.
Apply `hardo-brand` for context.

## Metrics that matter (priority order)

1. **Signups / link clicks** (north star) — track via the reply link with UTMs:
   `?utm_source=twitter&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=<name>`. Confirm signups in
   Hardo's analytics / Supabase.
2. **Profile clicks** — intent signal; people checking you out → potential followers.
3. **Bookmarks** — strong "this is genuinely useful / save-worthy" signal on X.
4. **Replies / meaningful conversations** — relationship + reach fuel.
5. **Follower growth** (esp. quality: finance/students) — compounding distribution.
6. **Engagement rate** = (likes + replies + reposts + bookmarks) / impressions.
   Rough benchmark: ~1-3% solid; threads & replies usually beat single tweets.
7. **Impressions** — context, not a goal by itself.

Vanity trap: chasing impressions/likes while profile-clicks, bookmarks, and signups
stay flat.

## Where to get the data

- **Per-tweet analytics:** "View analytics" under each tweet (impressions, engagements,
  detail expands, profile clicks, link clicks, bookmarks).
- **Account analytics:** X Analytics / Creator dashboard (impressions trend, top
  tweets, follower changes, audience).
- **Signups:** UTM links + Hardo analytics / Supabase.

## Tweet/thread scorecard

```
| Date | Pillar | Format (tweet/thread/QT/reply) | Hook type | Impressions | Eng.rate | Bookmarks | Replies | Profile clicks | Link clicks | Signups | Keep/Kill |
```
Review weekly (X moves fast). Most accounts find 1-2 formats/hooks carry results —
double down; cut the rest.

## Performance report (when asked to "report")

One-screen brief:
1. **Period + north-star metric.**
2. **Headline numbers** vs previous period (followers, impressions, eng. rate,
   profile clicks, link clicks, signups) with % change.
3. **Top 3 posts** (what they shared) + **bottom 2** (why they missed).
4. **2-3 insights** (meaning, not raw data).
5. **Next actions** (pillars/formats/cadence/reply targets to change).
Lead with insight; real numbers only.

## Competitor / FinTwit benchmarking

For other interview-prep / finance-career accounts & creators:
- Map their pillars, tweet vs thread mix, cadence, best hooks, and what gets
  bookmarked/reposted.
- Find gaps Hardo can own (e.g. they post motivation, not real technical reps +
  feedback → Hardo's wedge).
- Output: their strengths, their gaps, and **3 concrete openings for Hardo on X.**
Adapt patterns, don't copy. Label estimates; no fabricated benchmarks.

## Honesty rules

- Report real numbers only; if data's missing, say so and show how to get it.
- No invented benchmarks or testimonials.
