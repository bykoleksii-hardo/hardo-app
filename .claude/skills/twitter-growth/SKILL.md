# X / Twitter Growth & Promotion for Hardo App

On X, **replies and relationships drive growth more than your own tweets.** Always
apply `hardo-brand`.

## 1. Account optimization (do first)

- **Bio:** outcome-first + clear who-it's-for + CTA + link. Searchable keywords
  (investment banking, interview prep).
- **Pinned tweet:** your best thread or a crisp value tweet with a free-mock CTA.
- **Header + avatar:** clean, credible, value prop visible (use `design`/`ui-ux-pro-max`).
- **Name field:** "Name | Building Hardo (IB interview prep)".

## 2. The reply strategy (#1 growth engine on X)

- Build a list of **30-50 FinTwit accounts** your audience already follows: IB
  analysts/associates, finance-career creators, recruiters, student-finance accounts,
  "breaking into Wall Street" voices.
- **Reply early and add value** on their tweets (be in the first 10-20 replies on
  big accounts). Sharp, useful, additive — never "great post!" or a pitch.
- A strong reply on a large account can out-reach your own tweets 10-50x and convert
  curious profile-clickers into followers.
- Turn the best replies into standalone tweets.

## 3. Engagement & community

- **Engage back fast** — reply to everyone in your first hour; conversations beat likes.
- Use **quote-tweets** to add a take to trending finance/recruiting conversations.
- Run **engagement-bait done right:** genuine questions ("What IB technical scared
  you most?") that invite real replies.
- Consider **X Spaces** (audio) on recruiting topics — high trust, networking with
  FinTwit hosts.

## 4. Networking, partnerships, multipliers

- **Finance micro-creators** (students/recent analysts with engaged niche audiences)
  > big generic accounts. Co-create a thread, swap quote-tweets, do a Space together.
- The **Insider/Multiplier** persona on X = student finance societies, campus
  ambassadors, finance newsletter authors. Offer real value (free access for their
  audience during recruiting season). Draft personalized DMs (give me the handle/org).
- **Always disclose** sponsored/partner content.

## 5. Discoverability tactics

- Encourage **bookmarks and replies** explicitly ("bookmark this for your prep") —
  bookmarks are a strong quality signal on X.
- Keep outbound links out of high-reach tweets; link in the reply.
- Repurpose winners across formats and to LinkedIn/blog (`blog-repurpose`).
- 0-2 hashtags; rely on the network graph + replies, not hashtags.

## 6. Paid promotion (optional, later)

- Only **promote proven** organic tweets/threads. Don't pay to find message fit.
- Targeting: interests = finance/investing, followers-of relevant finance accounts,
  students/early-career. Start tiny, measure cost per signup, kill losers.
- Honest creative only — no job-outcome guarantees.

## 7. 30-day growth sprint (give when asked "how do I start")

- **Week 1:** optimize bio/pinned; build the 40-account reply list; 2 tweets/day +
  20 min/day replies.
- **Week 2:** publish first hero thread; start multiplier DMs (5 societies/creators).
- **Week 3:** first collab/Space or quote-tweet swap; double down on what's landing.
- **Week 4:** review metrics (`twitter-analytics`), cut flops, plan next month.

## Output format

Return a **prioritized "do this week" list** tied to the three personas and the
recruiting calendar, with any reply/DM copy drafted and ready to send. No spam;
disclose partnerships; never guarantee jobs.
