## Honest role: credibility layer, not a growth engine

A Company Page is what people check to see if you're real. It does **not** drive
distribution on its own — LinkedIn throttles Page reach versus personal profiles,
and a Page can't connect or DM. So: stand it up now for legitimacy + a home for the
link, but get **signups from societies (B2B2C) + SEO**, not from the Page feed.
Pair every push with a human (society share, ambassador, or an eventual named voice).
